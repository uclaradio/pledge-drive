UCLA Radio needs your help.

If UCLARadio were stuck on a desert island, the three things we would take with us are grit, passion, and music (and maybe some snacks). Now name the desert island “our depleting funds”, and suddenly we have a very real metaphor. UCLARadio has gotten through tough times with that magical trio, but what saves us time and again are listener donations. This year in particular, UCLARadio needs your help to afford new equipment so that DJs can provide essential tunes without mechanical mishaps, and new technology to expand our reach to the stars above

After 16 years on the internet and more than 50 years broadcasting, UCLARadio is asking for the help of its worldwide listeners to pitch in and listen up. And that means you.

To mark this monumental event, UCLARadio is broadcasting live 24 hours a day, Monday through Friday of this week. Tune in anytime (literally).
